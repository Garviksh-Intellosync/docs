# Review & Collaboration

## Third-Party Collaboration
- Send contracts to external parties using the built-in email feature.
- Receive updates and comments in real-time.

## Approval Process
- Define multi-level approval workflows.
- Assign specific approvers with different levels of permission.
- Track approvals via the dashboard.

## Digital Signing
- Choose from multiple e-signature options:
  - **Standard E-Signature**
  - **DSC (Digital Signature Certificate)**
  - **Aadhaar-based Authentication**
- Upload signed contracts and store them securely.
