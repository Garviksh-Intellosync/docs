# Contract Management

## Creating a Contract
- Click on **"New Contract"** to begin.
- Choose from pre-approved templates or create a custom contract.
- Add **Counterparties**: Search and invite new participants.
- Define contract terms, obligations, and key milestones.

## Contract Page Details
- **Contract Status:** Draft, Pending Review, Approved, Executed, etc.
- **Requesting Approvals:** Assign internal or external approvers.
- **Obligations & Compliance:** Track key deliverables and deadlines.
- **Activity Logs:** A detailed history of all modifications.
